About Me

Hi, I’m Steve Lemuel. Thank you for visiting my site!

I am a seasoned software engineer with a wealth of experience in the design and implementation of software systems, and in the leadership of development teams in a variety of organizational settings, both within established corporations and dynamic startups. My love for software engineering is unmatched, and it is not just limited to the practical aspects of my work. I am also driven to contemplate and reflect on the field, and to share my thoughts and experiences through writing. This combination of technical proficiency and intellectual curiosity has allowed me to excel in my career and to continuously evolve as a software professional.

Life is short, talk is not cheap.
